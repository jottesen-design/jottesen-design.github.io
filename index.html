About Me

Creating has always been my passion, whether it was painting vibrant scenes or making skate videos with friends. In college, I explored multiple majors, searching for the perfect fit. A pivotal conversation with a family friend introduced me to UX design. The blend of psychology and creativity was exactly what I had been seeking. I switched my major to Web Design and Development, and haven't looked back since.

Now, I combine my love for human-centered design with a dedication to crafting intuitive, engaging user experiences. Explore my portfolio to see how my journey and passion translate into my work. Let's connect and create something amazing together!

During my time as a designer, I’ve had experience in the following: 

– Running customer discovery
– Crafting research deck
– Running customer research workshops
– Crafting product visions
– Presenting product vision/roadmap to customer executives
– Collaborating on product requirements 
– Designing products for enterprise customers
– Utilizing and contributing to design systems
– Conducting competitive audit/analysis 
– Mentoring mid-level and junior designers

Approach

Peel the layers. Asking why consistently gives me closer to knowing the true problem whether with customers or stakeholders.

Know the landscape. Becoming an expert in the industry, product, and market competitors is key me bringing success to a product team.

Welcome critique. I’m always looking for fresh eyes on my work so that I can continue refining.  Sell the roadmap and vision. As a storyteller I love to selling the product vision to current or potential customers. Plus roadmap decks always need a little design live.
